Pomegranate Tequila

Mar Azul Blanco Tequila is 100% de Agave Tequila with natural flavors.

Craftsmanship

Mar Azul Blanco Tequila is hand-crafted using only premium select Weber Blue Agaves nourished in the rich volcanic soil of Jalisco, Mexico. Masterfully distilled using closely-held family recipes, this award-winning Blanco is "For sipping, not shooting."

Sensory Notes

  • AROMA: Rich notes of oak and vanilla, a hint of citrus.

  • TASTE: Complex, with smoky wood, vanilla and honey.

  • FINISH: Smooth, warm and lasting.

ACCOLADES

  • 2011 - Bronze Medal, San Francisco Wine and Spirits Competition

  • 2012 - Platinum Award and "Best of Show," World Spirits Competition
