Reposado

Craftsmanship

Mar Azul Reposado Tequila is hand-crafted using only the highest quality Blue Weber Agaves nourished in the rich volcanic soil of Jalisco, Mexico. Our Reposado is carefully aged in American White Oak barrels for at least six months to delicately enhance the rich natural tones and smoky wood flavor.

ACCOLADES:

2011-Silver Medal, San Francisco Wine and Spirits Competition

2012-Silver Medal, San Francisco Wine and Spirits Competition

Sensory Notes

  • AROMA: Pleasant notes of vanilla and caramel.

  • TASTE: Highly defined character, medium body, notes of wood, pear, vanilla and caramel. Smooth yet complex

  • FINISH: Warm, lasting finish.
