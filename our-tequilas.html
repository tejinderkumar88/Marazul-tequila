OUR TEQUILAS

Mar Azul takes pride in our promise to not use any chemical additives. We are a family company based in San Antonio with 13 years of experience in making Mar Azul Tequila. Our goal is to challenge and change the way consumers think about Tequila. We stand by our family values and are determined to provide a consistent quality handcrafted Tequila using only 100% natural ingredients.

Mar Azul is different! Our tequilas are made with the heart of the finest Blue Weber Agave and 100% natural ingredients. We cannot and would not take short-cuts to make our handcrafted tequilas. As a result, Mar Azul Tequila is a beautifully aromatic and stunningly smooth tequila with a rich vibrant taste and delightful finish. We invite you to taste the difference!

Quality

Mar Azul has taken hundreds of years of tradition and refined it to make the world’s best Tequila. Just as there are differences between “moonshine and fine sipping bourbon” there are differences between tequilas. We have found that when using 100% natural ingredients, we can consistently produce the finest quality tequila available today.

Mar Azul provides quality taste by committing to quality traditions.
